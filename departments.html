Information Technology

Information technology (IT) is the use of any computers, storage, networking and other physical devices, infrastructure and processes to create, process, store, secure and exchange all forms of electronic data.

Objectives

Information technology plays an important role in today’s business world. Majority of the companies rely on this for the purpose of data processing, fast communications and acquiring market intelligence. Information technology helps business improve the processes of business it drives revenue growth, helps them achieve cost efficiency and more importantly, ensures they increase revenue growth while maintaining a competitive edge in the market place. Five important objectives of IT are :

  • Integration of Stakeholders
  • Products Development
  • Process Improvement
  • Increased Cost Efficiency
  • Globalization

Scope of Employment

Wearing victory on the sleeves, Information Technology (IT) offers a promising career to the students. The undisputed king of engineering field, IT is growing tremendously with every passing year. However, there is no denial of the fact that this stream is not devoid of challenges.

Ranging from small offices to the global corporates, diploma IT engineers mark an important placement in every establishment. There is a huge requirement of IT professional in today’s era. The computer sector is the highly payable sector these days. At GPA College-the IT Engineering college there are many in campus job opportunities provided to the students to start their career before they complete their studies.

Student Outcomes

In GPA College-IT Engineering College Ahmedabad, the infrastructure and workstation are designed in such a way that the students of GPA College come out with these qualities:

  • Ability to tackle with the day to day digital needs of life.
  • Ability to apply the knowledge of mathematics and computing.
  • Ability to analyze the problem domain, identify and devise appropriate solutions.
  • Ability to analyze data, apply technical tools and comes to the required results.
  • Ability to lay strong foundation of innovative learning and creative ideas

Eligibility Criteria

For the purpose of admission to the Professional Diploma Courses, a candidate shall have passed the qualifying examination (SSC, STD.10) from, (i) The Gujarat Board; or (ii) The Central Board of Secondary Education Board (CBSE) or the Council of Indian School Certificate Examination, New Delhi or the International School Board or the National Institute of Open Schooling or equivalent, with minimum eligibility criteria of percentage of marks in subjects prescribed by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) from time to time.
